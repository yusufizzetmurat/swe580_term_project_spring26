## Observation About Contrastive Learning

The SimCSE paper makes a strong case that the *quality* of negatives matters more than the quantity. Hard negatives (semantically similar but not identical) force the model to make finer distinctions. For scientific paper similarity, two papers that cite the same works but are in different subfields are natural hard negatives.
