## Thoughts on [[Mixture_of_Experts]] and Our Work

The MoE connection to our paper: attention heads in a transformer are a soft form of mixture — each head specializes in different types of relationships. Our ablation study (removing individual heads) is essentially probing this specialization. This is the framing Professor Chen liked. I need to make this connection explicit in the paper.
