# Paper Reading Group - January 14

Weekly paper reading session. This week we covered flash attention and efficient transformers.

## Papers Discussed

1. Flash Attention: Fast and Memory-Efficient Exact Attention with IO-Awareness
2. Efficient Transformers: A Survey

## Key Takeaways

- Flash attention achieves exact computation with better memory usage by tiling the attention matrix
- The survey categorizes efficient attention methods into fixed patterns, learned patterns, and low-rank approaches
- Group discussed connections to [[Attention_Mechanisms]] and [[Self_Attention]]
